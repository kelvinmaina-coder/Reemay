Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, appointments, insurance, and more.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our reception at +1 (555) 123-4567, using our online booking system above, or visiting our hospital in person. Our staff will help you find the most convenient time with the appropriate specialist.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and many regional providers. Please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567 ext. 2 to verify if we accept your specific insurance plan.

For patients without insurance, we offer competitive self-pay rates and payment plans.

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Your insurance card and photo ID
  • Any relevant medical records or test results
  • A list of current medications with dosages
  • Payment method for any copay or deductible
  • Completed patient forms (if you received them beforehand)

Yes, we offer telemedicine services for many types of consultations. This allows you to meet with your doctor remotely via secure video conference. Not all medical concerns can be addressed through telemedicine, so please check with our staff when scheduling to determine if your appointment is suitable for a virtual visit.

General visiting hours are from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. For intensive care units (ICU), visiting hours are more restricted to ensure patient rest and recovery: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM. Special arrangements can sometimes be made for family members - please speak with the nursing staff.
