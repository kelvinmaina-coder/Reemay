Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, appointments, insurance, and more at EliteCare Medical Center.

You can schedule an appointment through multiple convenient methods:

  • Online: Use our appointment booking system above
  • Phone: Call our reception at +1 (555) 123-4567 (available 7AM-9PM daily)
  • In Person: Visit our main reception desk at 123 Medical Drive
  • Patient Portal: Existing patients can book through our secure online portal

Our staff will help you find the most convenient time with the appropriate specialist, typically within 1-3 business days for non-urgent appointments.

EliteCare Medical Center accepts most major insurance plans including:

  • Medicare
  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Humana
  • Kaiser Permanente
  • Many regional providers

Please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567 ext. 2 to verify if we accept your specific insurance plan. For patients without insurance, we offer competitive self-pay rates and flexible payment plans.

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Insurance card and photo ID
  • Any relevant medical records or test results from previous providers
  • A complete list of current medications with dosages (or the medications themselves)
  • Payment method for any copay or deductible
  • Completed patient forms (if you received them beforehand)
  • List of questions or concerns you'd like to discuss with your doctor

We recommend arriving 15 minutes early for your first appointment to complete any remaining paperwork.

Yes, EliteCare offers comprehensive telemedicine services for many types of consultations through our secure video platform. This allows you to meet with your doctor remotely from the comfort of your home.

Available for: Follow-up visits, medication management, chronic disease management, mental health consultations, and many non-emergency conditions.

Not suitable for: Emergencies, conditions requiring physical examination, or procedures. Please check with our staff when scheduling to determine if your appointment is suitable for a virtual visit.

General visiting hours: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily for most patient care units.

Intensive care units (ICU): More restricted to ensure patient rest and recovery - 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM (maximum 2 visitors at a time).

Pediatrics: Parents may stay 24/7; other visitors during general hours.

Special arrangements: Can sometimes be made for family members of critically ill patients - please speak with the nursing staff. All visitors must check in at the reception desk and may be screened for illness during flu season.
