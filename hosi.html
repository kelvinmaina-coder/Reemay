Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hospital management system

Is the system compliant with healthcare regulations?

Yes, our system is fully compliant with HIPAA, GDPR, and other major healthcare regulations. We implement strict security measures including data encryption, access controls, and audit logs to ensure patient data is always protected.

How long does implementation typically take?

Implementation time varies based on the size of your facility and which modules you're implementing. For most small to medium practices, we can complete implementation in 2-4 weeks. Larger hospitals typically take 6-8 weeks for full implementation and staff training.

Can the system integrate with our existing equipment?

Our system has built-in integrations with most major medical equipment and laboratory systems. We also offer custom integration services if you have specialized equipment. Our team will work with you to ensure seamless connectivity with all your existing systems.

What kind of training and support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive onboarding training for all staff members, along with detailed documentation and video tutorials. Our support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, and live chat. Higher-tier plans include dedicated account managers and on-site training options.

Is there a mobile app for doctors and patients?

Yes, our system includes native mobile apps for both iOS and Android. Doctors can access patient records, view schedules, and approve prescriptions on the go. Patients can book appointments, view test results, and communicate with their care team through the patient app.
